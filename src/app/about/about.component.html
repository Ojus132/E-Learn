E-Learn

About Us

This is a website made for PS1 internship at MyWebPartner by a team of 6 members from BITS Pilani. E-Learn in concept is an online educational platform. On it, instructors can add courses curated by them. Students can enroll in those courses, watch videos in them, solve quizzes and track their progress. Please take note that the website has been made as a part of a hackathon so not all features will be full fledged.
